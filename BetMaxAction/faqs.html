What Is Bet Max Action?
Bet Max Action is a first of its kind, tournament style online sports betting platform. We fused the best parts of Daily Fantasy Sports (DFS) & online sports betting to create a brand new way to get more action on sports! Ready. BET. Go!
How to Play?
First, you’ll need to pick your tournament. Find a price that works for you, and the sport(s) you want action on, and join! Everyone who joins that contest will start with the same Tournament Dollar ($TD) budget. Use the betting slate to pick your bets, the same way you would at a standard sportsbook. Everyone MUST risk their entire $TD budget in order to qualify for a prize! How you allocate your budget is entirely up to your strategy! By the end of the final whistle in the final game of that particular tournament, standings/payouts are determined by highest to lowest $TD balances at the end. Please see our rules section for full details.
